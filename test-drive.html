Load your content into your folders

Your system was built with a home for every kind of content. Tonight, drop what you've got into the right folder - your photos, your clips, your face - so tomorrow the content engine has real material to build with.

Where to find it: your Clinic folder lives in your home directory.
On Mac: Finder → menu bar → Go → Home → open Clinic → Assets.
On Windows: File Explorer → This PC → C:\Users\[your name]\Clinic\Assets.
Then just drag your files into the right folder below. Your skills read straight from here - no uploading, no linking.
Clinic › Assets › Photos
Every still image - photos of your clinic, your team, your treatments, your space, your products. Anything that isn't moving.
Feeds: cc-cover · cc-content-engine · cc-carousel
Clinic › Assets › Video
Short silent clips with no talking - the treatment room, machines running, hands working, product shots, the front door, any b-roll. The little pieces that get stitched together into a reel.
Feeds: cc-reelclips · cc-reelvoiceover · cc-reelassembly · cc-assemble · cc-find-clip
Clinic › Assets › Talking-Head
Any video of you or your team talking to camera - just filmed on your phone, rambling is fine. The system cuts the dead air, captions it and brands it for you.
Feeds: cc-reeltalkinghead
Clinic › Assets › Static
Anything already designed - finished graphics, logos in context, past posts you're proud of. Reference and reuse.
Reference library
Clinic › Business-Brain › brand-assets
Your logo (a transparent PNG) and your brand fonts, if you have them. Every skill that makes an image or reel reads your colours and font from here.
Feeds: everything that brands an image or reel

Not sure where something goes? If someone's talking, it's Talking-Head. If it's silent b-roll, it's Video. If it's a photo, it's Photos. When in doubt, just get it in - we'll sort it together tomorrow.
