Set it up · about 5 minutes

Connect Meta to Claude

This is Meta's own connector - no API keys, no developer account, no code. You paste one link and log in. It's free during Meta's open beta. Connect it READ-ONLY: that's where all the value is for a clinic, and it keeps you safe (more on that below).

The 5-minute connect

Do this in your own Claude account, on your own ad account. Read-only.

  1. In claude.ai, go to Settings → Connectors → Add custom connector.
  2. Name it Meta Ads and paste the connector URL (below).
  3. Complete the Facebook login and choose your ad account.
  4. Set the permission level to Read-only.
  5. Test it - ask Claude: "Show me my Meta ad accounts."
🔗 https://mcp.facebook.com/ads
Know the edges

What it can and can't do

The connector has 29 tools - 21 that read your data and 8 that can change your campaigns and spend money. For your clinic, the reading is the whole win. Here's the honest line on both.

✅ Use it for (read-only)

  • See which ads have the best hook rate and lowest cost per result
  • Spot creative that's fatiguing - frequency climbing, clicks dropping
  • Track cost per lead and spend trends week to week
  • Check your pixel and tracking are firing - the silent money-waster
  • Benchmark your CTR and CPM against your objective
  • Find which hook or angle the algorithm is rewarding right now

⛔ Don't rely on it for

  • Making the creative - it can't cut your video or write copy (that's the Multiplier and Advertising AI)
  • Compliance - it will happily push non-AHPRA copy if told to; the filter is still your gate
  • Hands-off launching - the write tools have no guardrails and can spend real money on a misread prompt
  • Real-time bid management - there's lag; it's not a bidding bot
  • Billing setup - that still lives in Ads Manager
🔒

Keep it read-only. Launching and budget changes stay a deliberate human step - your eyes on the money and the compliance. Meta's own advice is to start read-only and only consider the write tools after weeks of watching it behave. For a clinic, read-only does the job.

Google Ads - the search campaign

Meta finds people who don't know you yet; Google catches the ones already searching "lip filler near me" tonight. On Day 2 we build your search campaign ready to launch: /cc-keyword-research finds the exact phrases your patients type, then /cc-googlead-builder writes your full Responsive Search Ad set - 15 headlines, 4 descriptions, character-checked, AHPRA-screened - plus the Exact and Phrase match keyword plan to load with it. You paste it into Google Ads, set the daily budget, and it goes live the same day. No agency, no $99 "management fee", and every word is yours to change.

Once it's connected, this is your weekly five-minute check. Just ask Claude in plain English:
