Your skills - what each does + how to use it

Every skill installed on your machine, grouped by job. Tap a command to copy it, paste it into Claude Code, and add your own details. Build your Business Brain first - everything else reads it.

How to use any skill: open Claude Code in your ~/Clinic folder, type the command, and add plain-English detail after it. Every skill reads your Business Brain so it sounds like you, and everything is AHPRA-screened automatically. Run the two Foundation skills first - nothing else works properly until your brand guide exists.
Start here · Foundation - run these first, in order
/cc-resonance
The emotional interview. Claude interviews you and turns how your patients really feel into your messaging foundation (resonance-messaging.md).
Say: /cc-resonance for my clinic - then just answer the questions.
/cc-brand-guide
Your brand spine. Turns the resonance work into brand-guide.md - the one file every other skill reads first, so all your content sounds like you.
Say: /cc-brand-guide for my clinic - run it straight after resonance.
Content & social · a steady stream, in your voice
/cc-content-engine
A month of social content. Plans a month of posts across the four pillars (educate, connect, prove, offer), AHPRA-safe, and drafts them into your GHL Social Planner.
Say: /cc-content-engine build this month's content around [winter skin]
/cc-carousel
A teaching carousel. Slide-by-slide Instagram carousel copy in your voice, with a gentle CTA on the last slide.
Say: /cc-carousel a 5-slide carousel on [3 winter skin tips]
/cc-hook
Scroll-stopping first lines. Generates and scores hooks so your posts earn attention instead of getting scrolled past.
Say: /cc-hook 5 hooks for a post about [why a consult comes first]
/cc-marketing-genius
Sharper copy + strategy. Tightens subject lines, offers and the one CTA per message. Run it over any draft to lift it.
Say: /cc-marketing-genius sharpen this email: [paste]
/stoptheslop
Make it sound human. Strips the AI tells out of any draft and rewrites it in your brand voice. Run it before anything goes out.
Say: /stoptheslop rewrite this so it sounds like me: [paste]
Video & reels · from the clips in your Assets folder
/cc-reeltalkinghead
Talking-to-camera → a postable reel. Cuts the dead air, burns animated captions, writes the caption + hook + hashtags, and drafts it straight into GHL for approval.
Say: /cc-reeltalkinghead after you drop a clip in Assets/Talking-Head/
/cc-reelvoiceover
A voiceover montage. Assembles your short clips into one paced montage and writes the voiceover script timed to the cut, for you to record over.
Say: /cc-reelvoiceover with clips in Assets/Video/
/cc-reelclips
A trending-audio reel. Stitches your clips into one clean edit with no music baked in - so you add the trending sound natively in Instagram/TikTok where the reach is.
Say: /cc-reelclips stitch these for a trending sound
/cc-reelassembly
A story reel. Edits your clips in a told order (arrive → meet → explain → CTA card), captions the talking parts, and builds the full GHL post kit.
Say: /cc-reelassembly a welcome/journey reel from these clips
/cc-find-clip
The best moment from a long video. Finds and extracts the strongest ~2 minutes from a longer recording, ready for a reel.
Say: /cc-find-clip find the best clip in [file]
/cc-content-pipeline
Clip → finished social video. Takes a chosen clip and runs it through captions and branding into a ready-to-post video.
Say: /cc-content-pipeline turn this clip into a post
/cc-assemble
Stitch clips + music. Joins multiple clips into one reel with clean transitions and optional music.
Say: /cc-assemble stitch these clips into one reel
/cc-cover
A branded reel cover. A 1080x1920 cover in your colours and font with a punchy headline - the thumbnail for a reel or story.
Say: /cc-cover a cover with the headline [your line]
/cc-script
Video scripts + shot lists. Six script types (30/60-sec ad, problem-aware, offer, educational, remarketing), each with a 3-second hook, a talking-head script and a b-roll shot list.
Say: /cc-script give me the set for my front-door offer
Ads · compliant copy the agencies charge a fortune for
/cc-metaad-builder
Meta (Facebook/Instagram) ads. Headlines, neuro-emotive hooks and primary text variants, outcome-led and AHPRA-screened, ready for your campaign.
Say: /cc-metaad-builder Meta ads for my [offer]
/cc-googlead-builder
Google Search ads. 15 headlines, 4 descriptions, keyword and negative plans - all character-checked and compliance-screened, filling the RSA sheet.
Say: /cc-googlead-builder Google ads for [service] in [suburb]
/cc-ad-spy
See what competitors run. Researches the ads other clinics are running so you can position against them.
Say: /cc-ad-spy what are clinics near me advertising
Get found · so people find you on Google and in AI search
/cc-geo-seo
Show up in AI search. The moves to get your clinic cited when someone asks Google or ChatGPT for a clinic like yours, and to rank locally.
Say: /cc-geo-seo help me get found for [a skin clinic in my suburb]
/cc-keyword-research
What people actually search. High-value keywords with intent and difficulty, the base for your ads and SEO.
Say: /cc-keyword-research keywords for [treatment] in [suburb]
/cc-website-audit
Audit your website. Reviews your current site for what's helping and hurting bookings, with fixes.
Say: /cc-website-audit audit [your website URL]
/cc-landing-page
A landing page for your offer. Builds or audits a high-converting, AHPRA-safe page for one offer - paste it into GHL behind your ads.
Say: /cc-landing-page build a page for my [$199 offer]
Follow-up · the system that never drops a lead
/cc-follow-up-agent
Your whole follow-up nurture. Writes your enquiry ladder, welcome sequence, newsletter and win-back messages in your voice and loads them into GHL, so every enquiry is chased automatically.
Say: /cc-follow-up-agent for my clinic
