Install Claude Code - Mac

The app on your Mac that runs all your skills. It looks technical, but we do every step live together - here is what happens so nothing is a surprise.

15 minWe do it togetherMac (smoothest)

💡 What even is this?

Claude Code is a small app that lives on your Mac and runs your Clinic Catalyst skills - the content engine, the follow-up writer, all of it. Installing it is a few steps in a text window. You never do it alone; we run it on the day, screen by screen.

1

Have your accounts ready

Your Claude account (claude.ai) and your saved API key. That is all you bring.

2

Open Terminal

Press Cmd+Space, type "Terminal", hit enter. It is just a plain text box, nothing to break.

Cmd + Space → Terminal
3

Paste the one install command

We give you a single line to paste and press enter. It sets up Claude Code, the helper tools, and your Clinic Catalyst skills automatically.

It is one command, copy and paste - you do not type anything yourself.
4

Start it up

Type the word claude and press enter, then sign in with your Claude account.

type: claude
5

Paste your API key

When it asks, paste the key you saved. Done - your skills are now on your Mac.

That is the whole install 🎉

We run every step together on the day. You will never be stuck on a screen alone.

✅
You are done when: typing claude opens it and your Clinic Catalyst skills are listed. See you on the day.
