Cheat Sheet · How it works

Your content engine,
explained

One honest sentence covers it: the engine writes everything, and edits everything you record. What it never does is invent footage of your clinic. Here is the whole deal.

✅ What the engine DOES

  • 📝
    Writes all of it. A month of social posts, captions, emails, ad copy and video scripts at a time - built from your four content pillars.
  • 🎤
    Sounds like you. Every word comes from your Business Brain - your brand guide, your voice, your patients' actual concerns. No template-speak.
  • 🎬
    Edits what you record. Finds the best two minutes in your ramble, stitches clips, adds music, burns on word-by-word captions and a branded cover.
  • 🛡
    AHPRA-screens every line. Claims, guarantees, before-and-afters, banned words - checked before anything reaches a scheduler. AHPRA-compliant clinic marketing is the default, not an add-on.
  • 📥
    Lands drafts, not posts. Everything arrives in your GHL Social Planner as a draft waiting for your tick.

⛔ What it does NOT do

  • 🎥
    Invent video from nothing. It cannot conjure footage of your clinic, your hands or your patients. Real trust needs real faces - yours.
  • 🤖
    Fake results or people. No AI-generated patients, testimonials or before-and-afters. That is an AHPRA breach and a trust-killer, so it is built to refuse.
  • 🚀
    Post without you. Nothing goes live until you approve it. You are the editor-in-chief; the engine is the newsroom.
  • 👤
    Replace you. Patients book the person they already trust from the feed. The engine multiplies your face and voice - it never substitutes them.
Why this split matters: the slow part of content was never the filming - it was the writing, editing, captioning and scheduling. That is 90% of the hours, and that is the part the engine takes. Your part is a phone, decent light and two minutes of talking.
