Your daily routine

The system does the heavy lifting. Your job is a quick daily glance - about ten minutes - and a thumbs up. Here is the rhythm that keeps it all humming.

10 min a dayMostly approvingThe system does the rest

💡 The whole idea

You are not running software all day. The system drafts, schedules and follows up on its own. You just check in, approve what it has prepared, and get back to your clients. Ten minutes in, a full marketing and front-desk team worth of work out.

1

Morning brief (5 min)

Open Claude and ask for today: who is booked, who enquired overnight, what is queued. It hands you the day in plain English.

ask: what is on today?
2

Approve the replies

It has already drafted responses to enquiries in your voice. Read, tweak if you want, send. One tap each.

3

Glance at your content

A week of posts sits queued. Thumbs up, or ask for a tweak. No 11pm caption sessions.

4

Ask for anything, anytime

Drop a voice note or a line - write a post about X, reply to this lead, build me a page - and it just does it.

5

Once a month: pick your focus

Choose the month's offer or theme. That one decision points your ads, posts and emails the same way, automatically.

Ten minutes in, a team's work out 🎉

This is the part that gives you your evenings back. The system never forgets, never sleeps, and never sounds like a robot.

✅
You are doing it right when: checking in feels like a quick habit, not a chore - and things are getting done while you are with clients.
