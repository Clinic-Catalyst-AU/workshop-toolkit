💡 What even is this?

Your Business Brain is a single page that captures your voice, your services and how your clients feel. Every skill reads from it - so the content engine, the follow-up writer and your AI assistant all sound like you, not like a robot. Get this right and everything downstream is on-brand automatically.

1

Who we are

Your clinic, your location, what you do, and the one thing that makes you you. A few honest sentences.

2

How you sound (brand voice)

Your personality (warm? calm? never salesy), a couple of phrases you actually say, words you use, words you NEVER use (e.g. cheap, deal, anti-ageing, fix), and your sign-off.

House rules baked in: no emdashes, never overpromise a medical outcome, never sound like ChatGPT.
3

How your clients feel (the resonance layer)

The real person (one specific human, not a demographic), what they are feeling when they reach out, the shift you create (from how they arrive to how they leave), the words that open them up, and the words that shut them down.

This layer is what makes your copy land. It is the difference between on-brand and generic.
4

What you offer

Your services with prices, who each is for, your intro offer that gets new patients in the door, and any packages.

5

Who is who

Your team and their roles (so the assistant routes correctly), where your bookings live, and where enquiries come in (email, IG, FB, phone, website form).

Fill it once, it powers everything 🎉

We help you build this in the room - and the moment it is done, your whole system inherits your voice.
