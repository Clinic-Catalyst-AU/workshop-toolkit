Your pre-work · do these in order, before Tuesday
1

Update your laptop

Tonight, on good wifi, plugged in. Mac: System Settings > General > Software Update (macOS 14+ is golden). Windows: Settings > Windows Update, install everything, restart, repeat until "You're up to date". Know your login password - the install will ask for it.

Windows only: also install Git for Windows - run it and click Next through every screen. Mac doesn't need this.
2

Create your Claude account

Sign up at claude.ai with your clinic email. This is the assistant you'll be approving and driving from minute one on Tuesday.

Sign up - use this link
3

Make your API key and save it

Five-minute job in the Anthropic Console, saved somewhere safe. You'll paste it in once on Tuesday.

See the API key guide →
4

Start your GHL free trial

Start the trial and claim your phone number early - it takes time to activate. Then, while logged in, open the calendar snapshot link: one click loads our pre-built calendar and booking setup into your account.

Start the trial Then load the calendar snapshot →
5

Download your business registration PDF

Free from ABN Lookup - we lodge your SMS bundle with it first thing Day 1.

abr.business.gov.au
6

Pack your content goldmine - four folders

On the laptop you're bringing, make four folders and fill them with whatever you have:

1. Photos - your clinic, rooms, staff, products, machines, you with staff or clients.
2. Video - no talking - raw clips: treatments happening, hands working, machines running, a walk through the clinic.
3. Talking head - any video of you speaking to camera, even rough takes.
4. Brand - logo files, brand colours (hex codes or a screenshot), font names, any style guide.

Do not polish anything. Raw is exactly right - the system does the editing, captions and branding. AirDrop it off your phone before you come. More is better.
7

Laptop charged, charger packed, questions jotted down

Restart your laptop Tuesday morning. Anything you're unsure about, write it on a sticky note and bring it - we cover every bit of setup live together.
