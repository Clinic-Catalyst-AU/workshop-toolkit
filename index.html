Day 1 - Build the system

Attract + convert · 9:00am - 4:00pm
  • Welcome + how your AI system works. Kelly walks you through the system you're about to own, while the install runs in the background on every machine.
  • Install Claude Code first. You approve and drive it from your own keyboard - it's the brain of everything that follows.

  • Your Meta campaign goes live overnight. Real data by morning.

Day 2 - Automate + get found

Nurture + scale · 9:00am - 4:00pm
  • Read last night's ad data honestly - what to scale, what to kill.
  • Content engine on. A month of on-brand social from your own footage and photos - the folders you packed.
  • Follow-up that never sleeps. The nurture ladder and newsletter loaded into GHL, in your voice.
  • Get found - SEO + AI search. So when someone asks Google or ChatGPT for a clinic like yours, you come up.
  • Take it for a test drive. Hands on your own wheel before you leave.
  • Go live. Everything switched on, tested, and yours - with your daily 10-minute routine to run it.
