Optional · only if you keep an external booking system

Calendar Mirror /cc-calendar-mirror

You probably won't need this. If you build your rooms, machines and services natively in GHL, clients book there, everything runs off that one calendar, and there is nothing to mirror. That is the no-brainer - one less subscription, one less thing to break.

Only if you decide to keep your old booking system (Timely, Cliniko or Fresha) alongside GHL: this is the one agent we build live, together, on the day - because it is the only piece that needs your own GHL webhook and your own key. It watches GHL and copies every confirmed booking across, so the two calendars never disagree.

Worth knowing: tools that give us a key (Timely, Cliniko) sync in the cloud, fully hands-off. Fresha has no key, so its bridge only runs while your computer is on and needs babysitting - bookings queue and catch up, never lost. One more reason to just go native in GHL. We build this one live with you on the day if you want it.
